# Stream Timer Extension ⏱️

A lightweight browser extension designed to help you manage your binge-watching habits. Keep track of your screen time and automatically set boundaries on your favorite streaming platforms.

## 📺 Supported Platforms

- Netflix
- Amazon Prime Video
- Disney+
- YouTube

## ✨ Features

- **Custom Time Limits**: Set a precise time limit (in minutes) for your viewing session directly from the extension popup.
- **Quick Presets**: Easily choose from predefined timers like 15m, 30m, 45m, or 1h.
- **Dynamic Theme**: The extension's UI automatically adapts its colors to match the streaming service you are currently watching.
- **Live Notifications**: Get a 1-minute warning toast notification before your time runs out.
- **Automatic Pause**: When your timer expires, the extension automatically pauses the video and displays a full-screen overlay.
- **Extend or Exit**: Once the time is up, you can choose to extend your session by 15 minutes or immediately close the tab.
- **Daily Tracker**: Keeps a running total of the time you spend streaming each day per platform.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar while on a supported streaming site.
2. Enter your desired time limit or click one of the quick preset buttons.
3. Click **Start Timer**. 
4. Sit back and enjoy! The timer will run quietly in the background and notify you when your time is up.

## 🛠️ Installation (Developer Mode)

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open Chrome (or your Chromium-based browser) and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **Developer Mode** in the top right corner.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the directory containing the extension files.
